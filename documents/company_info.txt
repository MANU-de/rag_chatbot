Innovatech Solutions: Company Overview

Innovatech Solutions, founded in 2015, is a leader in enterprise AI software.
Our mission is to empower businesses with cutting-edge artificial intelligence.
We offer a suite of products including:
- InsightEngine: For predictive analytics.
- AutomataPlatform: For workflow automation.
- VisionAI: For computer vision tasks.
Our headquarters are located in San Francisco, California.
The CEO is Johnathan Chen. We value innovation, collaboration, and customer success.
